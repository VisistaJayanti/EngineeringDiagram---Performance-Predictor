ISO 286 TOLERANCE GRADES AND APPLICATIONS

IT5
Application: precision spindle bearings, gauge manufacture.
Typical tolerance for 25mm diameter: 0.009mm.
Achievable by: grinding, precision boring, honing.

IT6
Application: precision fits, rolling bearing seats, precision gears.
Typical tolerance for 25mm diameter: 0.013mm.
Achievable by: grinding, fine boring, precision reaming.
Common fit designations: H6, g6, h6, k6, n6, p6.

IT7
Application: general precision engineering, most common precision grade.
Typical tolerance for 25mm diameter: 0.021mm.
Achievable by: reaming, boring, careful turning.
Common fit designations: H7, f7, g6, h6, k6, n6, p6.
H7 hole: standard clearance and transition fit hole tolerance.
H7/g6: sliding fit — shafts that slide but do not rotate under load.
H7/h6: close running fit — accurate location with small clearance.
H7/k6: transition fit — accurate location, possible slight interference.
H7/n6: transition fit — more interference, light press fit.
H7/p6: interference fit — light press fit for permanent assembly.

IT8
Application: general engineering, non-precision fits.
Typical tolerance for 25mm diameter: 0.033mm.
Achievable by: careful drilling, standard reaming, turning.
Common fit designations: H8, f8, e8.
H8/f8: easy running fit — for shafts running in plain bearings.

IT9
Application: general workshop practice.
Typical tolerance for 25mm diameter: 0.052mm.
Achievable by: standard drilling, turning.

IT10
Application: rough machining, where fit accuracy is not critical.
Achievable by: standard milling, drilling.

IT11
Application: very rough work, cast surfaces, flame cut edges.
Achievable by: drilling without reaming, rough milling.

IT12
Application: raw stock tolerances, sheet metal stamping.

SURFACE FINISH AND MANUFACTURING PROCESS RELATIONSHIP
Ra 0.1 to 0.2: lapping, superfinishing
Ra 0.2 to 0.4: cylindrical grinding, honing
Ra 0.4 to 0.8: fine grinding, precision boring
Ra 0.8 to 1.6: reaming, fine turning, fine boring
Ra 1.6 to 3.2: turning, milling, boring, drilling with reaming
Ra 3.2 to 6.3: standard turning, milling, drilling
Ra 6.3 to 12.5: rough turning, rough milling, drilling
