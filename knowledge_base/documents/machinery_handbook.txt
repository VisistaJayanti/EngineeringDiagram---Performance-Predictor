MACHINERY HANDBOOK EXCERPTS

DRILLING SPEEDS AND FEEDS
For carbon steel HB 180-220:
    HSS drill: cutting speed 20-25 m/min
    Carbide drill: cutting speed 60-80 m/min
    Feed per revolution: 0.1 to 0.3 mm depending on diameter
    Smaller diameters use lower feed rates.

REAMING ALLOWANCES
Leave 0.2mm on diameter for reaming up to 20mm hole.
Leave 0.3mm on diameter for reaming 20mm to 50mm hole.
Reaming speed is one third of drilling speed.

TAPPING
Tap drill = nominal diameter minus pitch.
M8 x 1.25: tap drill = 6.75mm
M10 x 1.5: tap drill = 8.5mm
M12 x 1.75: tap drill = 10.25mm
M16 x 2.0: tap drill = 14.0mm
M20 x 2.5: tap drill = 17.5mm

BORING
Single point boring achieves IT6 to IT8.
Boring bar overhang should not exceed 4 times bar diameter.
Finishing cuts: depth 0.1 to 0.3mm, feed 0.05 to 0.15mm/rev.
