SURFACE FINISH REQUIREMENTS AND PROCESSES

Ra 0.1 micrometres
Process: superfinishing, lapping
Application: precision sealing surfaces, optical surfaces

Ra 0.2 micrometres
Process: cylindrical grinding, precision honing
Application: hydraulic cylinder bores, precision bearing bores

Ra 0.4 micrometres
Process: fine grinding, precision boring
Application: bearing seats, precision shafts

Ra 0.8 micrometres
Process: fine boring, reaming, precision turning
Application: general precision fits, O-ring grooves

Ra 1.6 micrometres
Process: reaming, careful boring, fine turning
Application: most precision engineering fits H7 and better
This is the most common precision requirement in general engineering.

Ra 3.2 micrometres
Process: standard turning, milling, boring
Application: general machined surfaces, non-sealing faces

Ra 6.3 micrometres
Process: rough turning, drilling, rough milling
Application: non-critical surfaces, clearance faces

Ra 12.5 micrometres
Process: rough machining, coarse milling
Application: non-functional surfaces

THREAD SURFACE FINISH
Metric threads M series: Ra 1.6 to 3.2 standard
Fine threads: Ra 0.8 to 1.6 recommended

O-RING GROOVE SURFACE FINISH
Dynamic seals: Ra 0.4 to 0.8 micrometres
Static seals: Ra 0.8 to 1.6 micrometres
